About Me

In a sentence

A marketer who thinks in strategy, makes things carefully, and waters her plants inconsistently.

Hi — I'm Grace. I've spent 7 years building brands and running campaigns across Singapore, Thailand, and Indonesia. I've worked in defence comms, energy, ed-tech, and SaaS — which sounds scattered, but the through-line is always the same: figure out what actually makes people pay attention, and then make that.

I like the full stack of marketing — the strategy and the spreadsheet, the campaign brief and the caption. I'm comfortable owning a budget, managing an agency, and writing the copy myself when it needs to be just right.

Outside of that, I make things with my hands, move my body, and attempt to maintain a small collection of plants that are, at best, tolerating me.
